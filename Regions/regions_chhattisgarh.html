Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is a central Indian state filled with dense forests, beautiful waterfalls, and superb temples.

Chhattisgarh is sometimes nicknamed the "rice bowl" of India due to its immense agricultural production of rice. It also has numerous ways of cooking rice, and rice is a staple food item eaten alongside many of its dishes.
