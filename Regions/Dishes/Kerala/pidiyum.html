Pidiyum

Pidi is a comforting dish made of spherical rice dumplings simmered in aromatics, popular within the Christian community.

The name “pidi” means small and describes the size of these dumplings. It is typically enjoyed with a variety of curry or gravy dishes, particularly with chicken curry called pidiyum kozhiyum. It can also be served sweet with coconut milk, jaggery, and cardamon. While there are many different types of pidi, the most popular version is pidi made with a thick gravy base. Pidi is a culturally significant dish, often being served at special occasions or festivals.
