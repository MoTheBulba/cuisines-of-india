Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh, located in northern India, is known for its rich history and architectural wonders, and is particularly famed for the birthplace of the deities Lord Rama and Lord Krishna.

Uttar Pradesh's cuisine can broadly be categorised into four main subsets: Awadhi, Mughlai, Bhojpuri, and local native cuisines. Awadhi and Mughlai cuisines have rich, meat-centric dishes, often prepared with elaborate techniques and fragrant spices. On the other hand, Bhojpuri cuisine tends to be milder, featuring a wide array of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.
