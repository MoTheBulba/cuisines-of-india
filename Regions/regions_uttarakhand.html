Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, nestled in northern India amidst the majestic Himalayas, is renowned for its spiritual significance, breathtaking landscapes, and rich biodiversity.

The cuisine of Uttarakhand reflects the abundance of local produce, where lentils feature prominently in Uttarakhand's dishes, alongside a variety of other grains and local ingredients. Uttarakhand's cuisine can be broadly categorised into two main categories: Garhwali and Kumaoni cuisines.

While both share many common dishes, each region has its own unique culinary identity with subtle variations in ingredients and cooking methods. Uttarakhand's cuisine tends to be simple yet nutritionally dense and incredibly flavourful.
