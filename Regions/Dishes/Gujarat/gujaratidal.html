Gujarati Dal

Gujarati dal is a general name for a variety of different lentil curries from Gujurat.

One of the most common recipes offers a thick, sweet, and tangy dal with a smooth texture. It is known for its complex and satisfying flavours, including nuttiness from peanuts and spiciness from chillies.

Kokum is typically used for the tangy flavour, but lemon juice or tamarind can be used as a substitute. This version of Gujarati dal is best enjoyed with chapati but can also be served with plain rice.
