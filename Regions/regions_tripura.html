Tripura

Situated in northeastern India, Tripura is known for its picturesque hills and exquisite handicrafts crafted from hand-woven cotton and bamboo.

While Tripura's cuisine is often described as mild due to its minimal use of spices, it often uses a generous number of green chillies, lending a pleasant heat to many dishes. Perhaps the local chillies are mild or maybe Tripura locals have a high tolerance for spiciness. Either way, the Tripura cuisine offers a wonderful array of dishes where flavours commonly come from the aforementioned chillies, garlic, ginger, and fermented fish.

The cuisine also relies on fresh, local produce that change with the season. From smoking and drying techniques to steaming and roasting, Tripura's cuisine uses a diverse range of cooking methods to create dishes that are as vibrant and diverse as the landscapes that inspire them.
