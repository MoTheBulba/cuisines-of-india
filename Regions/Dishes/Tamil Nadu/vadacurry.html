Vada Curry

Vada curry is a delicious and comforting Tamil dish made with lentil fritters in a spicy, coconut curry.

The “vada”, or lentil fritters, has few ingredients and is beautifully crispy and golden-brown. The vada can be enjoyed by itself as snack with coconut chutney and tea, but the vada can be transformed into a satisfying meal by making it into a curry. It is best served with idli or dosa but can also be eaten with chapati or rice.
