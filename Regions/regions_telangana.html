Telangana

Telangana, a state in southern India, is renowned for its globally famous film industry and historical landmarks.

The cuisine of Telangana has strong tangy and spicy flavours from common ingredients like tamarind and chillies. Staple foods include rice, lentils, millets, and nuts, which lend richness and heartiness to many traditional dishes.
