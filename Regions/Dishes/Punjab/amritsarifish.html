Amritsari Fish

Amritsari fish is a fried fish recipe from the culinary hub of Amritsar in Punjab.

The dish involves pan-frying masala-marinated fish until it becomes aromatic and crispy. The fish can be made into fritters, known as pakoras, by deep-frying them for an equally crispy but more indulgent result. A popular street food, Amritsari fish can be enjoyed with some chutney and a squeeze of lemon, or served as a flavourful side dish alongside a main meal.
