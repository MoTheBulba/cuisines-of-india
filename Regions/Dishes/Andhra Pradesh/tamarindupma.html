Pulusu Pindi

Pulusu Pindi, known by various names such as uppindi, puli upma, and tamarind upma, essentially refers to a tangy or sour upma. It is made from ground rice and tamarind cooked with tempered spices.

The dish has many variations and may also be made from semolina or lentils, or even a combination of semolina, lentils, or rice. This light dish is popularly consumed for breakfast and can be enjoyed on its own or eaten with some South Indian chutney or pickle*.
