Chandigarh

Chandigarh, an Indian Union Territory and the shared capital of Punjab and Haryana, holds a unique position in India's history and urban planning. Established as a planned city post-independence, Chandigarh stands as a symbol for India's freedom and its future as an independent nation. Designed by the Swiss-French architect Le Corbusier, the city boasts spacious green areas, orderly neighbourhoods, and a vibrant community spirit.
