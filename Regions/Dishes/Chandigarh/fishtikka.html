Fish Tikka

Fish tikka is a dish where small, marinated fish pieces are grilled until perfectly crispy and juicy.

This quick and simple dish is bursting with flavour, making it a popular street snack too. The marinade for fish tikka typically includes yogurt, mustard oil, and a blend of spices that gives a beautiful red colour to the dish. Fish tikka pairs well with chutney as a wonderful snack or serves as a flavourful side to complement other dishes.
