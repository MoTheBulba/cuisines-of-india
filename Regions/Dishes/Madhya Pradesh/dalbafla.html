Dal Bafla

Dal bafla is a hearty dish made from boiled and baked bread rolls served with spicy dal.

The bread rolls (i.e., the bafla) should have a satisfying crunch on the outside, and a soft, fluffy texture on the inside. The ghee poured on top of the bafla adds richness and compliments the spiciness of the dal. This is an incredibly comforting, filling, and warming dish, perfect for the cold weather.
