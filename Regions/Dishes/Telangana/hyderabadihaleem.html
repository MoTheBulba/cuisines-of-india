Hyderabadi Haleem

Hyderabadi haleem* is a multi-grain, multi-lentil, mutton dish that holds a Geographical Indication (GI) tag, signifying its cultural and regional significance.

Only those authorised or residing within the designated geographic location are permitted to use the product name. Hyderabadi haleem is a hearty and comforting dish that has several steps and takes hours to make. It is a culturally significant dish made during special occasions and best enjoyed with a flatbread.
