Macher Jhol

Macher jhol is a Bengali fish curry typically made with freshwater fish and seasoned liberally with aromatics and spices.

There are many variations of this recipe and is enjoyed across India, particularly in West India and the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. While this dish uses few ingredients and is quick to make, Macher jhol is a delicious and satisfying dish. It is best served with plain rice.
