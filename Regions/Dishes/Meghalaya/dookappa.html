Do'o Kappa

Do'o kappa is a chicken dish originating from the Garo community within Meghalaya.

Known for its heat, this dish is cooked with a generous amount of green chillies, with some recipes calling for as many as 15-20 chillies. However, adjustments can be made to suit varying spice preferences. Notably, no added oil is used and relies solely on the chicken's rendered fat for cooking. A unique ingredient is kalchi, a type of cooking soda derived from banana or cotton ash, although baking soda is a suitable substitute.
