Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi biryani is one of India's most renowned dishes, and is a deeply satisfying meal.

This biriyani can be cooked in one of two ways: the kacchi style (semi-cooked rice and raw meat are cooked together) or pakki style (semi-cooked rice and raw meat are cooked separately). Both styles take a long time to prepare. The kacchi style in particular can be challenging to do, especially for first-time cooks.

While traditionally made with mutton, chicken or lamb are also popular. Hyderabadi biryani is often found at gatherings and special occasions, highlighting its culturally significance.
