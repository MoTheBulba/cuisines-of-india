Bhutte Ka Kees

Bhutte ka kees is a popular street food from Madya Pradesh, made with grated corn, milk, and tempered spices.

It is a flavourful dish that can be made quickly in 15 minutes with few ingredients. The sweetness from the corn, the creaminess from the milk, and the aromatic spices come together to create a delightful flavour profile. Bhutte ka kees can be eaten by itself or as a side.
