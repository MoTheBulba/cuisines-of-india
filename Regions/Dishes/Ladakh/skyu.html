Skyu

Skyu is a traditional hearty pasta soup hailing from Ladakh that consists of stir-fried vegetables, spices, milk, and pasta made from atta flour.

The pasta is especially distinctive because of its little dimples. Skyu's quick preparation and warming nature makes it a convenient and satisfying meal, particularly during the colder months. This dish typically uses local, seasonal vegetables, and is often enjoyed by itself or alongside other dishes.
