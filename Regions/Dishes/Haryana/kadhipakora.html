Kadhi Pakora

Kadhi pakora is a classic yogurt-based curry with onion fritters, aptly named for its two main components: “kadhi,” referring to the yogurt-based curry, and “pakora” meaning fritter.

The sweet pakoras soak up the tangy curry that results in a beautifully flavourful dish. Kadhi pakora is a traditional Punjabi dish but it is also commonly eaten in Haryana too. Easy to prepare with many variations in recipes, it is best served with rice or flatbread.
