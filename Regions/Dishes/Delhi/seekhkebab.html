Seekh Kebab

Seekh kebab is type of meat kebab shaped into long cylindrical tubes and grilled.

A popular Delhi variety is made with mutton seasoned with chaat masala and topped with ghee. Seekh kebabs is made with finely blended meat that gives it a smooth texture. It is best enjoyed with chutney, and can be paired with rice or any type of flatbread.
