Khaman/Nylon Khaman/Dhokla

Khaman, nylon khaman, and dhokla all refer to a steamed savoury cake made topped with a spicy, tempered syrup.

The cake itself is plain and is transformed into a delicious treat when the syrup is poured over it. It is best enjoyed with chutney and tea.

Each variation uses different ingredients for the cake, yet they all use the same cooking method: steaming the batter and topping it with a tempered syrup.

  1. Nylon khaman is made with fine gram flour, giving it a soft and spongy texture
  2. Khaman uses ground chana dal and offers a slightly textured cake
  3. Dhokla is made with ground rice and urad dal, that gives it a more savoury and textured taste
