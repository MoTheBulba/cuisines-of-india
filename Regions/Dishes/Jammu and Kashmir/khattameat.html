Khatta Meat

Khatta meat is a tangy and hearty mutton curry from the Dogra community in Jammu.

The distinct tanginess of the curry, reflected in its name 'khatta' meaning sour or tangy, is achieved by adding either amchur (dried mango powder) or anardana (pomegranate). Traditionally, anardana is used and the dish is smoked. Simple to make, yet it requires a long cooking time to ensure the meat tender. It pairs perfectly with rice, or enjoyed with gheur, a local fried savoury pancake with a mesh-like structure.
