Daler Bora

Daler bora, or lentil fritters, is a delicious crispy snack in Bengali cuisine.

Made from a batter of blended soaked red lentils, onions, green chilies, and spices, these fritters are a comforting and indulgent snack. Traditionally, they are fried in mustard oil, but if mustard oil is unavailable, you can either add mustard to the lentil batter or fry the lentils with mustard seeds, ensuring to use a flavourless oil as a substitute for mustard oil.
