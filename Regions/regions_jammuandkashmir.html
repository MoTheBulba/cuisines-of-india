Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir is a northern Indian union territory, home to breathtaking landscapes adorned with beautiful valleys and lakes.

Its rich and diverse cuisine is influenced by the region's abundance of fresh, local ingredients. The cuisine can be broadly categorised into Jammu and Kashmiri cuisines. Jammu cuisine is notable for its preference for sour and tangy flavours and includes a mix of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes.

On the other hand, Kashmiri cuisine is more meat heavy. Within the Jammu and Kashmiri cuisine, a myriad of communities inhabit these region contribute their own food culture, such as the Dogri community in Jammu and the Kashmiri Pandits in Kashmir.
