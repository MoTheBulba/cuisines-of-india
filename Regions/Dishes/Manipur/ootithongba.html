Manipur Kangshoi

Ooti thongba is a comforting peas and vegetable curry in Manipur.

The dish uses baking soda to help soften the peas that results in a tender texture. Various vegetables can be added such as bamboo shoot or raw papaya, and is best served with plain rice. Ooti Thongba is traditionally cooked over a wood fire, imparting a delightful smoky flavour to the curry, and is best enjoyed with plain rice.
