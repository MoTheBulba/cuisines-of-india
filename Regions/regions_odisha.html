Odisha

Odisha is an eastern Indian state known for its rich ancient history and architectural wonders.

Many of its dishes includes seafood and vegetables, with flavours that leans towards a balance of sweet and sour flavours rather than intense spiciness compared to many other Indian cuisines. One notable aspect of Odisha's cuisine is its moderate use of oil and uses on a diverse array lentils and starchy vegetables or fruits, resulting in dishes that are not overly heavy yet still leaves you satisfyingly full.
