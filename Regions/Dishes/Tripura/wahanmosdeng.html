Wahan Mosdeng

Wahan mosdeng is a tasty pork salad popular in Tripura. This dish uses raw aromatics, commonly red onions, ginger, green chillies, and culantro â€” an herb with robust flavour and larger leaves compared to its coriander relative.

It is a very easy dish to make, where the only cooking involved is the gentle simmering of pork until tender. Some variations include cooking the aromatics with the pork as a stir-fry. Wahan mosdeng is typically served with rice or chapati.
