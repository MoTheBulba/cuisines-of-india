Patta Chicken

Patta chicken, also known as jungle or junglee chicken, is a tribal dish where chicken pieces are marinated in a coriander masala, then carefully wrapped in leaves before being cooked over an open fire.

While modern variations may incorporate ingredients like tomatoes, garam masala, and chili powder, traditional recipes often adhere to a simpler flavour profile, relying on a handful of local spices and ingredients. The cooking process imparts a heavenly aroma to the dish as the flavours meld together within the leaves. Patta chicken is typically enjoyed alongside rice rotis or plain rice.
