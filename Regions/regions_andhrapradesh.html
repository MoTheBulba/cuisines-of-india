Andhra Pradesh

Andhra cuisine encompasses diverse regional variations, notably coastal, Rayalaseema, and Hyderabadi cuisines. Coastal Andhra Pradesh prominently includes seafood, while Rayalaseema is distinguished by its liberal use of fiery red chillies. Hyderabadi cuisine, influenced by Mughlai culinary traditions, is known for its rich and aromatic dishes, often prepared with indulgent ingredients and elaborate cooking techniques.
