Kumaoni Raita

Kumaoni raita is a refreshing yogurt-based side dish originating from the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand.

It features grated cucumber, spices, mustard oil, and fresh coriander. The grated cucumber brings a light and refreshing quality to the raita, while the mustard oil adds depth and complexity. Kumaoni raita can be eaten with anything, and makes a quick and light snack on its own.
