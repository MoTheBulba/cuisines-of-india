Pondicherry Fish Assad Curry

Pondicherry fish assad curry is a delectable, aromatic fish curry prepared with a base of coconut and posto (poppy seed paste), prepared with whole spices, and finished with a squeeze of lime juice.

It is typically served with plain rice or crusty bread that allows it to soak up its gravy. Quick to prepare, it showcases the multicultural background of Puducherry, particularly highlighting its fusion of French and Tamil cuisine.
