Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh is a central Indian state steeped in cultural and spiritual heritage, and offers a varied cuisine influenced by its neighbouring states and regional culture.

Madhya Pradesh's cuisine has a wide array of flavours and ingredients, where preference for certain foods varies across different regions within the state. For example, wheat is more prevalent in the north and rice is enjoyed more in the south. Whether exploring the bustling markets of its vibrant cities or savouring traditional dishes in rural villages, the state is a fantastic place for foodies.
