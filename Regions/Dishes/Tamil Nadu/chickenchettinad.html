Chicken Chettinad

Chicken chettinad is an exceptionally flavourful chicken curry from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu, famous for its rich non-veg dishes.

In this dish, marinated chicken is cooked in a spiced (but not spicy) coconut-based curry. One of its important ingredients is a local lichen called black stone flower that brings a complex and uniquely savoury taste to the dish. While this ingredient can be hard to find and many recipes omit this ingredient, it is highly recommended to use if accessible to fully experience the excellence of chicken chettinad.
