Punjab

Punjab, a state in northern India, is known for its illustrious history, cultural heritage, and incredible food.

Renowned worldwide, Punjab's cuisine include iconic dishes such as butter chicken, tandoori chicken, tikka masala, and chana masala, and more. Rooted in a rich history dating back thousands of years, Punjab's cuisine reflects influences from Middle Eastern countries and the Mughal era, as well as the region's fertile lands that yield abundant produce. This diverse culinary landscape led to a spectrum of flavours and preparations, ranging from indulgent to rustic and nutritious dishes. Punjab's cuisine has thus emerged as a beacon of culinary excellence.
